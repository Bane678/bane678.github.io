Our Approach

Our Process

How We Match You With The Right Candidate

In-Depth Consultation

We learn about your family, lifestyle, household structure and specific requirements.

Curated Shortlist

We present a carefully selected shortlist of pre-vetted candidates who meet your criteria.

Interviews & Trials

We coordinate interviews and, where appropriate, arrange trial periods for best fit.

Ongoing Support

After placement, we remain available for ongoing guidance and support throughout the arrangement.

Screening Standards

Rigorous Vetting You Can Trust

Every candidate undergoes our comprehensive screening process before being presented to your family.

Background Checks

Enhanced DBS checks, international criminal record screening and identity verification.

Reference Verification

Thorough verification of employment history with detailed reference calls for every position held.

In-Person Interviews

Detailed face-to-face interviews assessing personality, experience, presentation and suitability.

Qualification Checks

Verification of all stated qualifications, certifications and professional training credentials.

Right to Work

Full documentation checks ensuring legal right to work in your country of residence.

Confidentiality

Full NDAs and confidentiality agreements to protect your family's privacy at all times.
