About Us

A Legacy of Trusted Recruitment

For over 25 years, Hyde Park International has set the standard for elite household staff placement worldwide.

Our Story

Founded on Trust, Built on Excellence

Hyde Park International was established in London with a single purpose: to provide distinguished families with the most exceptional household professionals available.

From our offices in Mayfair, we have grown into a globally recognised recruitment agency trusted by royal households, diplomatic families, UHNW individuals and prominent business leaders across more than 40 countries.

Our approach is simple — we treat every placement as a matter of trust. We listen carefully, vet thoroughly and match precisely. Our reputation has been built one successful placement at a time.

2001

Founded in London

Established in Mayfair as a boutique nanny recruitment agency serving London families.

2008

International Expansion

Expanded services to the Middle East, Europe and North America, building a global candidate network.

2015

Full Household Staffing

Broadened our services to include housekeepers, estate managers, chauffeurs and full household teams.

2026

25 Years of Excellence

3,000+ successful placements across 40+ countries, and a 98% client satisfaction rate.
